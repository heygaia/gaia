## Welcome to GAIA

The fastest way to experience GAIA is through our hosted service. No installation, no setup - just sign up and start using your personal AI assistant.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit heygaia.io">
    Navigate to [heygaia.io](https://heygaia.io) in your web browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up with your email address or use one of our social login options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Chatting">
    Once logged in, you can immediately start interacting with your AI
    assistant.
  </Step>
</Steps>
